Tampa Turtles!

Hours: 49

  • Being a part of Alternative Breaks, I had to go on a weekend trip to volunteer in Tampa. More information about Alternative Breaks is available on the organizations page. On this trip we went to Tampa Florida from December 15, 2024 to December 18, 2024. On the 15th we explored Tampa. On the 16th, we volunteered with The Samaritans Purse. We helped make a womans house livable after Being devistated by Hurricane Milton. This experience helped me learn that if I am more fortunate than others, it does not take a lot of effort to help others who are not as fortunate. On the 17th, we volunteered with The Salvation Army. We helped prepare gifts for underprivlaged families. This experience helped me learn that I do not have to spend any money just to help people who do not have disposable cash. Spending money seems to hold people back from helping in their community, but it does not have to cost a single cent.
