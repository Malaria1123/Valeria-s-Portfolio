Planned Parenthood Generation Action (UM)

November 2025

  • Planned Parenthood Generation Action is a club that advocates for female reproductive health. I was loosely involved with the club this past term but will be more active in the next terms to come. This club is very meaningful to me because gynecology is the specialty I want to find a career in as a doctor. In this club, we do tabling to raise awareness, hand out free products (merchandise and reproductive health products) to students at the University of Miami and host events for the students to get involved. Some of these events include trivia nights and care package packing events. This club helped me find amazing people to connect with after a rough transition into the University of Miami as a transfer student.

    • Init (FIU)

This showed me how computer science can be useful in a variety of areas and not strictly for software engineers.

    • American Medical Student Association (FIU)

    August 2024 - December 2024

  • I was the most active member of pre-med AMSA for the fall 2024 term at Florida International University. At AMSA, students have the opportunity to get community service hours, donate to various causes, attend countless medical school application events, and get hands on experience with many medical trainings. I attended a phlebotomy workshop, a CPR workshop, a manual vacuum respiration workshop, a suturing workshop, and many other types of introductory training. The more events attended, the more points the members got. I was a gold distinction member (the highest level recognized) with over 50 points for the term. This organization introduced me to so many medical experiences, showed me several opportunities for pursing medicine and helped me give back to the community from school.
