American Medical Student Assosiation

August 2024 - December 2024

  • I was the most active member of pre-med AMSA for the fall 2024 term at Florida International University. At AMSA students have the oppurtunity to get community service hours, donate to various causes, attend countless medical school application events, and get hands on experience with many medical trainings. I attended a phlebotomy workshop, CPR workshop, manual vacuum respiration workshop, suturing workshop and many other types of introductory training. The more events attended, the more points the members get. I was a gold distinction member (the highest level recognized) with over 50 points for the term. This organization introduced me to so many medical experiences, showed me various oppurtuinites for pursing medicine and helped me give back to the community from school.
